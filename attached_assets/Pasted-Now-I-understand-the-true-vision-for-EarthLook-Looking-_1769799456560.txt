## ⚠️ **ETHICAL CONSIDERATIONS**

### **Must Address:**
1. **Data Privacy**
   - Identity data is extremely sensitive
   - Let users control what's stored vs. calculated on-device
   - GDPR/CCPA compliance for personal data

2. **Bias in Algorithms**
   - Who decides what makes a city "good" for each identity?
   - Avoid reinforcing segregation
   - Consult with communities you're scoring for

3. **Safety Concerns**
   - Showing hate crime data could frighten users
   - Balance honesty with hope
   - Always provide context and resources

4. **Stereotyping Risk**
   - Don't assume all people of one identity want the same things
   - Customization is key
   - Let users define their own priorities
